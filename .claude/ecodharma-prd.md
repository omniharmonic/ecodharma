# EcoDharma

> *The thing the world most needs from you is the thing you are uniquely able to give — offered in a way that creates upward spirals.*

## 0. TL;DR

EcoDharma takes multiple "readings" of a person — Western and Vedic astrology, Human Design, Gene Keys, and an Ikigai reflection — and interprets them, in a distinctive voice, **through a framework of regenerative civilization distilled from Benjamin Life's body of writing.** The output is not a horoscope. It is a living map of how a particular person can most meaningfully *plug into the regenerative transition*: their unique gifts, the **trim-tab actions** where those gifts create disproportionate leverage, and the collaborators with whom they form generative **constellations**.

It is the technological completion of the **kinship journey** — the return leg of the hero's journey, where the gift won in the depths is offered back in service of life, and where that offering can only be made *in relationship*.

This document specifies v1 and stages the larger vision into phases. It is offered as a foundation for the build, with my own judgment calls flagged explicitly for your review in §15.

---

## 1. Vision & premise

We are living through what Joanna Macy named the **Great Turning** — the shift from an industrial growth society to a life-sustaining civilization. Your corpus argues this transition will not be won by reform but by *building parallel systems with their own compounding logic* — re/acc's upward spirals, cooperative federation, bioregional finance, open protocols, and the inner work of the Earth dharma. The transition needs an enormous diversity of contributions, and it needs them *coordinated* — not centrally planned, but woven, the way an ecosystem is woven.

The bottleneck is not a shortage of willing people. It is a **legibility and coordination problem**. People who want to serve the Great Turning don't know *where* their particular medicine is needed, *who* to weave with, or *how* their gifts compound rather than compete. Most "find your purpose" tools stop at the individual. Most "find collaborators" tools stop at skills-matching. Neither holds the whole: gift × need × relationship × place, oriented toward regeneration.

EcoDharma exists to make the kinship journey *navigable at scale*. It refracts the universal light through the unique prism of a person — as your Earth Dharma essay puts it — and then shows them the specific shape their participation in the new-and-ancient story wants to take.

**Core premise (the EcoDharma thesis):**
1. Each person carries unique gifts, discoverable through multiple complementary lenses.
2. The regenerative transition has discernible **domains** and **trim-tab actions** where leverage concentrates.
3. The highest contribution lives at the **intersection** of a person's gifts and the transition's needs — and it must be structured as a *positive feedback loop*, not a one-off act.
4. This contribution is only fully realized **in constellation** with complementary others.

The opportunity is to build the *connective tissue* of the regenerative movement — a tool that is, in your own words, "viral regeneration": knowledge flowing globally through a commons while implementation stays local and relational.

Early cohort assumption: your existing network and readership (regenerative practitioners, web3/regen builders, conscious-culture community). *Open question §15-A: who exactly is cohort zero?*

**Secondary:**

**Phase 2+:**

## 4. Product principles

1. **Mythopoetic, not predictive.** EcoDharma is a meaning-making instrument, not a forecasting engine. We say so plainly. These systems are lenses for self-reflection and orientation; they are not empirically validated, and the honesty makes the tool *more* powerful, not less. *(Confirmed direction.)*
2. **Consent-first.** No one's profile is ever used to assess collaboration without their active, explicit opt-in. This is architected from line one, not retrofitted.
3. **Gifts over deficits.** The tool describes what a person uniquely *offers*, and how to relate to their shadow — never a ranking of worth.
4. **Complementarity over conformity.** Constellations seek alignment where gifts are complementary, "rather than expecting each other to do things we were not uniquely capable of doing."
5. **Commons by design.** The distilled framework, once made, is governed as a commons (CC BY-SA). The code is open source. Paid features cover compute, not gatekeep wisdom.
6. **Fellow traveler, not guru.** The voice is a way-shower walking beside you, never a preachy oracle.
7. **Place-aware.** Bioregion and place are first-class context, consistent with the bioregional spine of your work.

## 5. The framework — the secret sauce

This is the heart of the product, and the part that must be built *first*. Everything else is plumbing around it.

### 5.1 What the framework is

A structured, versioned artifact — distilled from your **entire written corpus** (the project essays) **and wiki.omniharmonic.com** — that defines the **building blocks of a regenerative civilization and transition**. It is the lens through which every reading is interpreted and every match is made.

### 5.2 Proposed shape *(my synthesis — for your review, §15-B)*

I propose the framework be organized as a matrix of two axes, joined by a third element:

**Axis 1 — Regenerative Domains** (the "what the world needs" axis): the arenas where the Great Turning is being built. Drawn directly from your corpus, a first-pass set:

- **Inner / Consciousness** — Earth dharma, reparenting, healing trauma, secure attachment, sovereign unity, sacred ecology
- **Relational / Kinship** — conscious relating, leaderful spaces, the infrastructure of belonging, community weaving
- **Ecological** — watershed restoration, regenerative land, bioregional stewardship
- **Economic** — cooperatives, solidarity squads, upward-spiral economics, local currency, bioregional finance, post-capitalist VC
- **Governance / Civic** — polycentric and place-based governance, civic localism, protocols of place
- **Technological / Protocol** — d/acc defensive tech, open protocols, cosmolocalism, the mycelial sensing of networks
- **Cultural / Story / Ritual** — the new-and-ancient story, dance, initiation, the regenerative narrative

**Axis 2 — Gifts & Archetypes** (the "what you uniquely offer" axis): a taxonomy of contribution-types, synthesized from (a) the modality readings, (b) Joe Lightfoot's regenerative archetypes (credited, then **transcended and included** into something more expansive), and (c) your own writing on roles in the transition. These are *ways of serving*, not job titles — e.g. the Healer, the Weaver, the Builder, the Steward, the Storyteller, the Protocol-smith, the Convener, the Composter, the Way-shower. *(Final set emerges from the distillation swarm.)*

**The connective element — Trim Tabs** (Buckminster Fuller's image, central to your re/acc work): the small, high-leverage actions that, taken at the intersection of a person's gifts and a domain's need, **create upward spirals** — self-amplifying feedback loops that compound regeneration and outpace extraction. A trim tab is the unit of the suggestion engine's output: *"Given who you are, here is a small action in this domain that creates disproportionate, compounding leverage."*

**The validator — Ikigai.** Overlaid on every proposed trim tab as a four-fold check: does this sit where what you **love**, what you're **good at**, what the **world needs**, and what can **sustain you** (livelihood) overlap? Ikigai keeps the matching honest, alive, and economically viable — not just aspirational.

So the core output is: **Gift × Domain → Trim-tab action, validated by Ikigai, structured as an upward spiral.** That is "ecodharma" rendered computable.

### 5.3 How the framework gets made (the swarm)

A one-time, re-runnable **offline distillation pipeline** (not a runtime feature) reads the corpus and produces the framework artifact:

1. Ingest the full corpus (project essays + wiki).
2. An agent swarm extracts: domains, sub-domains, archetypes/gifts, trim-tab patterns, key concepts, and the relationships between them.
3. Synthesize into a structured, versioned artifact (the "Framework Artifact" — see architecture doc).
4. You review and refine; the artifact is the source of truth for interpretation and matching.
5. Versioned so that profiles can be regenerated when the framework evolves.

### 5.4 Governance

Frozen enough to be stable per release; living across releases. Made first, then **governed as a commons** with transparent versioning and an editorial process to be defined post-v1.

## 6. The modalities

Five lenses, each feeding structured data + context into the interpretation engine.

| Modality | What we compute | Sourcing | IP posture |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Western (tropical) astrology** | Full natal chart as raw JSON | Open-source Swiss Ephemeris library, self-hosted (the Golden Calf library) | Clean — astronomical facts |
| **Vedic (sidereal) astrology** | Sidereal chart w/ ayanamsa, as JSON | Same engine, sidereal mode | Clean |
| **Human Design** | Bodygraph: type, profile, authority, gates, channels, centers — from ephemeris | Open-source MIT library, self-hosted | Compute ourselves; original interpretation only |
| **Gene Keys** | Core sequences (gate/line positions) — same gate math as HD | Derived from the HD gate computation | Compute ourselves; **never reproduce Rudd's copyrighted text** — generate original interpretation, link out to genekeys.com for canonical contemplation |
| **Ikigai** | Four-fold self-reflection (love / skill / world-needs / livelihood) | Guided questionnaire, optionally Claude-assisted | N/A |

**Inputs:** users supply birth **date, time, and location**. Location resolves to lat/long/timezone.

**Interpretation strategy (confirmed):** feed **raw JSON chart data** to the Claude interpreter with as much context as each modality needs to be legible. For traditional systems the model already knows the vocabulary (north node, Saturn return, etc.); for non-traditional systems (Gene Keys especially) we supply **as much source/contextual scaffolding as possible** so the model can interpret faithfully *in original language* without reproducing proprietary text.

**The birth-time problem (must-handle):** many users won't know their exact birth time, which degrades rising sign, houses, and — critically — Human Design (minutes can change type/profile). Handling:
- Make time optional but clearly flag what becomes uncertain without it.
- Offer a "no-time" fallback (solar/whole-sign chart; HD shown with a confidence caveat).
- Clearly label which insights are time-dependent so users know what to trust.
- *(Phase 2: lightweight rectification via life-event questions.)*

**IP boundary (confirmed "don't push it legally"):** we use the trademarked system *names* nominatively only, compute all positions from open ephemeris, and generate **original** interpretation in the EcoDharma voice. We do not reproduce Gene Keys or Human Design proprietary descriptive text. Western/Vedic astrology carries no such constraint.

## 7. Scope

### 7.1 v1 — MVP (the spine)

The minimum that delivers the core magic: *reading → framework mapping → gift profile → constellation.*

1. **Onboarding & birth data capture** (date/time/location, with the time-handling above).
2. **Reading ingestion** — compute all available modality charts to JSON and persist.
3. **Gift Profile generation** — Claude interprets the combined readings *through the framework* and in the EcoDharma voice, producing:
   - The person's **unique gifts**
   - Their natural **domains** of contribution
   - Suggested **trim-tab actions** (gift × domain), Ikigai-validated
   - **Shadow / things to avoid** and how to relate to them
   - Profiles are **persistent and consistent** (regenerated only on explicit request or framework version bump).
4. **User-composed Constellations** — a user invites others (who have consented) into a constellation and receives:
   - One-to-one synastry: combined dynamics, complementarities, frictions
   - One-to-many: constellation-level read of collective gifts, gaps, and "what to make explicit"
   - Collaboration guidance: how each relates differently, how worldviews shift, how to weave
5. **Active-consent layer** — explicit opt-in before any profile is used in another's constellation.
6. **Skills / offerings on the profile** (lightweight, seeds the future marketplace).

### 7.2 Phase 2

- **Astrological forecast & nudge engine** — transit computation + push notifications describing what's live *and how it affects each person's specific constellations and relationships.*
- **Marketplace v1** — gift-aligned matching between people and projects/orgs/teams (the "job board that finds work aligned with your gifts").

### 7.3 Phase 3 / deferred *(noted per your request)*

- IP-sensitive deepening pending any future licensing (richer Gene Keys / HD content) — **not pursued now.**
- Additional modalities only if they earn their place (explicitly **not** Myers-Briggs).
- Rectification tooling for unknown birth times.
- Deeper Joe Lightfoot archetype collaboration.
- Bioregional/place-based constellation discovery.
- Name/trademark clearance for "EcoDharma" before any public branding *(see §15-D)*.

## 8. Core user journeys

**Journey A — The kinship journey (individual):**
Arrive → enter birth data → readings computed → receive Gift Profile in the EcoDharma voice → see your domains and trim-tab actions → add your offerings → invited to find your people.

**Journey B — Constellation composition:**
Have a profile → invite collaborators (they consent) → compose a constellation → receive the collective read (complementarities, frictions, what to make explicit) → use it to weave more effectively.

Opt into forecasts → receive a nudge when an astrological event meaningfully touches you or a constellation → with concrete relational guidance.

**Journey D — (Phase 2) Finding your work:**
List offerings → the marketplace surfaces projects whose needs match your gifts → connect.

## 9. The EcoDharma voice

The interpreter speaks as a single coherent persona — call it the **EcoDharma voice** — modeled on **Aiden Cinnamon Tea**: relational rather than transactional, "sweet and sharp," a *mirror and weaver of paradoxes* rather than an oracle dispensing answers. Layered with your spec, the voice is:

- A **complexity-science nerd** (feedback loops, emergence, systems)
- An **immanent-metaphysics & ecological-spirituality nerd** (interbeing, sacred ecology, embeddedness — without requiring belief)
- A **solarpunk / living-systems nerd** (regeneration, upward spirals, the world we're building)
- **Deep but never preachy** — a *fellow traveler and way-shower*, offering reflections and invitations, holding uncertainty, never moralizing or flattering.

It treats the person as kin in a living web, not a user to optimize. It invites contemplation; it does not prescribe identity. *(The voice spec lives as a versioned system prompt — see architecture doc.)*

## 10. Consent & privacy

The single most important design decision. *(Confirmed: active consent required.)*

- A person's readings and profile are **private by default.**
- To appear in someone else's constellation, a person must **actively opt in** — granular, revocable, per-relationship or per-constellation.
- No silent use of another's data, ever. No scraping profiles into constellations without a yes.
- Birth data is sensitive PII; encrypted at rest, minimized, deletable.
- Clear data-ownership and export. If this runs on Parachute, the same consent rules apply to vault data.

## 11. Monetization

Free as far as possible; charged only where AI compute is heavy. *(Confirmed.)*

- **Free:** core Gift Profile, a bounded number of constellations, basic offerings.
- **Paid (compute-driven):** deep multi-modal synthesis, large/unlimited constellations, forecast nudges, re-generations, org/team features.
- Open source + commons-governed framework means paid tiers fund infrastructure, never gate the wisdom itself.

---

## 12. Governance as commons

Once distilled, the framework is released to the commons under CC BY-SA, with transparent versioning. A lightweight stewardship process (to be defined post-v1) governs framework evolution so it stays coherent, plural, and non-dogmatic — consistent with the Earth dharma's insistence that it "must not become a religion."

## 13. Success metrics

- **Resonance:** % of users who rate their Gift Profile as "accurate and useful" (qualitative + thumbs).
- **Activation:** % who take or commit to a suggested trim-tab action.
- **Constellation formation:** number of consented constellations composed; reported collaboration outcomes.
- **Return / depth:** profile revisits, forecast opt-ins (phase 2).
- **Commons health:** framework forks/adaptations, contributions back.

*(Guard against vanity metrics; the real signal is people actually weaving and acting.)*

---

## 14. Risks & open questions

- **Framework quality risk** — if the distillation is shallow, the whole product is shallow. Mitigation: invest most heavily here; human-curate the swarm output.
- **Epistemic credibility** — mythopoetic framing must be explicit to avoid pseudoscience critique.
- **Pseudo-precision of matching** — constellation reads must invite reflection, not assert deterministic compatibility.
- **AI cost** — multi-modal synthesis is token-heavy; the free/paid line and caching must be deliberate (see architecture §AI cost).
- **Name collision** — "EcoDharma" is in use by existing eco-Buddhist orgs; clear before branding.
- **Birth-time data quality** — see §6.
